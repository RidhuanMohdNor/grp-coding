About Nevoks Fit






Nevoks Fit was established in 2021 to introduce jerseys and services that we will provide to residents in the area of
Section 15, Bandar Baru Bangi Selangor. We provide and sell jerseys of various designs
and colours that are interesting and abstract. Furthermore, Nevoks Fit also provides services for customers who want to modify their shirts
such as adding the name and number of the player on the back of the shirt. We also provide jerseys of various sizes where it starts from size S to 9 XL
and it will make customers have many comfortable size options according to customer satisfaction.


Nevoks Fit is our business name and it is easy to understand by people around the Bandar Baru Bangi area.
Nevoks is a name we created ourselves and we agreed to use it because it sounds fierce and elegant at the same time.
In addition, we also added "Fit" behind the word Nevoks because it will make people aware that the company Nevoks Fit
provides services or products related to sports and extreme activities.
